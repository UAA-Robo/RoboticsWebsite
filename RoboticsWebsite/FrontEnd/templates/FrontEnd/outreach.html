We are invested in bringing STEM to the Alaskan community, especially focusing on K-12 students. Throughout the year, we bring our robots to events in the community and help out with workshops.

If you have information about an event or opportunity that we can contribute to, please reach out to us at uaa_icebergrobotics@alaska.edu

Since Fall 2022, we've reached...
400+ youth
200+ guardians
60+ UAA Students

Local Events

UAA STEM Day
1 Oct 2022

We ran a booth where we let youth control an arduino car and Sphero robots in a maze. The Nerds of the North also joined us and brought one their robots to show off.

UAA Haunted Halloween Night
29 Oct 2022

For our booth, youth could control an arduino car or Sphero robots in a maze. They could win candy and 3D-printed minion prizes!

UAA Getaway
9 Dec 2022

We brought our VEX Robot to this event so that UAA Students could drive it and shoot disks. (Photo taken by Taylor Heckart, a Northern Light News reporter).

Workshops

Soldering Workshop

We partnered with the UAA Society of Women Engineers to teach UAA Students how to solder. All 40 of the sign up spots were filled.

3D Printing Workshop

We again partnered with the UAA Society of Women Engineers to teach 3D Printing.
