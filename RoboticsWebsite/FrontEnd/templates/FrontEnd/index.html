Welcome to UAA Iceberg Robotics ...

Competed in the 2023 VEX U World Championship as ICE1!

We are UAA's College Robotics Team in Anchorage, Alaska. We embrace all types of creativity. If you're interested in robotics, engineering, programming, marketing, or project management, we're the place for you! You don't have to know anything about robotics to join us. You just have to be willing to learn!

During the pandemic, the club came to a standstill and most of the robotics members graduated, taking their knowledge with them. We’ve had to start from square one this season. However, with the support of our fellow students across many different majors (engineering, computer science, business, etc) we’ve been able to grow club membership from less than 5 to over 20 members. Now we have a team of committed members ready to build robots and win competitions.
